The Bloc Québécois is a Quebec separatist political party that wishes to create a new French country that is welcoming, more ecological, defending equality in diversity, based off the rule of law, and secular. It is currently led by Yves-François Blanchet, who, according to his bio, believes that Ottawa wants to encroach on the will of the people of Quebec.

Due to the party's beliefs, the party only operates in the province of Quebec. As of May 14th, 2026, the Bloc has 22 MPs in the House of Commons. To me, the party seems to have many historical errors and propaganda, including saying that Ottawa "failed in its attempt to find a formula that met the minimum demands made by Quebec to reinstate the Canadian Constitution." This is contradicted by the fact that Quebec was the last province that held out of the Constitution, and the fact that *all* provinces needed to be on board, not just QC.

## Platforms & Values

According to the [Choisir le Quebec: Plateforme politique 2025](https://www.blocquebecois.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/blocqcplateforme-2025web.pdf) platform on blocquebcois.org, the Bloc Québécois values:
- Countering Trump
- Better Employment & Labour
- Quebec-first trade
- Be secular
- Français
- Separate from Canada
