## Platforms & Values

So, the Conservative manifesto is stupidly hard to find and looks incredibly boring, but according to the [CONSERVATIVE PARTY OF CANADA POLICY DECLARATION](https://cpcassets.conservative.ca/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/27094930/45977d2d3ad26ec.pdf) platform on conservative.ca, the PCC/CPC values:
- Government Accountability
- Democratic Reform
- Open Federalism
- Tax Relief and Simplification
- Economic Development
- Trade
- Justice
