The leader of the Liberal Party is [Mark Carney](pm.gc.ca) since March 9, 2025, who is the current prime minister. The Liberal Party of Canada is the country's main centre-left-leaning political organization, with the Conservative Party of Canada, their main opponents, being mainly right-wing, and the NDP to their left. It is the oldest currently active federal political party in the country, and has dominated the federal politics of Canada for much of its history.

After the [2025 election](https://newsinteractives.cbc.ca/elections/federal/2025/results/), the Liberals won the election, marking another Liberal government in power. However, they [didn't receive a majority initially](https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/livestory/byelections-terrebonne-university-rosedale-scarborough-southwest-9.7162168), only achieving that later through **floor-crossers** (such as MPs d'Entremont and Ma) and winning by-elections. This gave them 174 seats in the House of Commons, already a majority government above the minimum 172 seat-threshold to secure a majority government.

## Platforms & Values

According to the [Canada Strong](https://liberal.ca/cstrong/) platform on liberal.ca, the Liberal party values are:

- Unify all Canada
- Defending our Sovereignty
- Safeguarding Canadian Values
- Building the Economy
- **NEW** Standing up to Trump
- Free Press
