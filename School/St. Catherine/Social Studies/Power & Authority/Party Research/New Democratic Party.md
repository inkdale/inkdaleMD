The leader of the New Democratic Party is [Avi Lewis](https://www.ndp.ca/avilewis) since March 29, 2026. The NDP/NPD is a centre-left to left leaning party with the original goal of overhauling capitalism to replace with socialism, toning it down as of late. Canada has never had an NDP PM.

After the [2025 election](https://newsinteractives.cbc.ca/elections/federal/2025/results/), the NDP lost its official party status, as a party is required to have at least 12 Members of the House of Commons in order to be a "recognized party" for the purposes of parliamentary proceedings. The NDP ended up with a grand total of 5 seats, which made it the fourth-largest party in the House of Commons.

## Platforms & Values

According to the [Policy of the NDP](https://www.ndp.ca/sites/all/themes/canadandp.themes/canadandp-convention2023/blocks/block--home-footer/docs/2024-04-11_Policy-EN-2023-V2.pdf) platform .pdf on ndp.ca, the New Democratic Party of Canada values:

- Innovating in our Energy and Economy
- Building a Clean & Sustainable Canada 
- A Canada Where We *All* Rise Together
- Solidifying Canada's Place in the World
- Making Canada Fair
- Human Rights
