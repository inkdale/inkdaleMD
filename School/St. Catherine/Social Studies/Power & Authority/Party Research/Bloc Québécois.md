The Bloc Québécois is a Quebec separatist political party that wishes to create a new French country that is welcoming, more ecological, defending equality in diversity, based off the rule of law, and secular. It is currently led by Yves-François Blanchet, who, according to his bio, believes that Ottawa wants to encroach on the will of the people of Quebec.

Due to the party's beliefs, the party only operates in the province of Quebec. As of May 14th, 2026, the Bloc has 22 MPs in the House of Commons.
