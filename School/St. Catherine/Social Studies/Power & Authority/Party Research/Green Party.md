The Green Party of Canada, led by [Elizabeth May,](https://www.greenparty.ca/en/elizabeth)is the country's main environmentalist party. [Founded in 1983 at Carleton University](https://www.greenparty.ca/en/our-history), the leader, Dr. Trevor Hancock ran 60 candidates in the 1984 federal election, gaining momentum and recognition. The Green Party is self-described as neither left nor right, although their policies lean more traditional left, in my opinion.

According to the website of the House of Commons, the Green Party has a singular seat in the House of Commons, occupied by Elizabeth May. This makes them a [non-recognized party for the purposes of parliamentary proceedings](https://www.ourcommons.ca/members/en/party-standings). The Green Party also operates a youth arm, the Young Greens.

## Platforms & Values

According to the [Green Party 2025 Election Platform](https://cdn.greenparty.ca/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/GP_Platform_English_Final-1.pdf) platform on greenparty.ca, the Green Party values:
- Fair Taxation
- Better Employment & Labour
- Canada-first trade
- Affordable Housing
- Climate Action
- Healthcare
